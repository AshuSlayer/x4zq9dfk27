Background

Today, we live in a world where if you can press buttons on a computer keyboard you are considered talent. A world in which singers that cannot actually sing without the aid of 'engineering magic' are praised for their abilities and rewarded handsomely.

A Far Cry

What a far cry from the days when blistering guitar solos ruled the airwaves, a time where true talent was acknowledged and appreciated. A time when guitarists took risks and dared to push the boundaries of their instrument.

Restoration of a Golden Era

Shred is more than just a technical or advanced style of playing; it embodies the search for the guitar's inner soul. However, in recent years, both Rock music and Shred have seemingly taken a backseat in the mainstream media. Despite what the critics say, we know that Rock and Shred are very much alive and well. As an evangelist of this genre, I am committed to igniting a movement aimed at the complete restoration of the former glory of Rock and Metal.
